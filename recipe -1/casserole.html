Casserole Recipe

Description

This cheesy delicacy is sure to leave your family full and stuffed, Creamy chicken, mushroom, and a variety of cheese this dish is simple and delicious

Ingredients

  • No Yolks Broad Egg White Noodles, 12 ounce poly bag
  • 2 cans of cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 cans of cream of celery soup
  • 2 cans of cream of chicken
  • 2 cans of white chicken meat or pack of chicken breast
  • 2-3 bags of cheddar cheese (or any cheese to your liking)
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • Preferred seasoning

Directions

  1. Put 4 cups of water to boil in a pot(add a cap full of oil*opt*)
  2. Strain the water from the pasta and pour pasta into seperate bowl
  3. Add ingredients(cream of mushroom,chicken,celery,can of chicken,preffered seasoning, 1/2 cup of milk, 1/2 of the cheese you have)
  4. Mix thorougly
  5. Set oven to 325 degrees
  6. Place mix into oven safe container and add the rest of the cheese on top
  7. place into the oven for 30 minutes or until desired results
  8. Take out and serve!
